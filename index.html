Ready to start? Here’s how:

Step 1: Enroll

Decide which type of service animal you would like to train and purchase the training course. The registration form will be sent to you via email and you can start your online training.

Step 2: Study and Practice

You will finish your training sessions and complete the assignments at the end of each session. Your tutor will review your progress and grade your performance.

Step 3: Certification

After your finish all the assignments with a passing grade, SDTSI will issue you a digital or paper certificate to vertify that you and your dog have finished the online service dog training course.

Any individual getting assitance from a dog that performs tasks or does work directly related to a physical or psychological condition may qualify for a Service Dog.

Visible or Non-visible Disabilities that may qualify for service animals include but are not limited to:

PTSD

Diabetes

Mobility Limitation

Seizures

Panic Attack

Stability

Autism

Or any other Physical

or Psychological Disability
